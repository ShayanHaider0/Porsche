News & Events

Porsche Achieves Record $40.22 Billion Revenue in 2022, Fueling Growth Ahead of Planned IPO:

Porsche issued its global 2022 results, with record revenues driving growth, especially in territories like the U.S. and China. The company's global revenue for 2022 hit a record $40.22 billion, up 13.6% from a year ago, with operating profit jumping over 27% to $7.27 billion1. Volkswagen is aiming for a valuation of up to €75 billion for Porsche when the luxury brand is floated on the stock market.

Formula E and Motorsport Success:

Porsche has been performing strongly in motorsport. The Porsche Penske Motorsport team celebrated a successful season in the FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC), securing seven out of eight possible titles. The team’s drivers clinched the World Championship at the final race in Bahrain.

Taycan Update:

Porsche has recently completed the second generation of its Taycan electric vehicle, which continues to be a central part of their electric vehicle strategy​.
